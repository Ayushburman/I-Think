1. What are Vitamins?

Vitamins are organic compounds your body needs in small amounts to function properly.

They help in:
Growth
Immunity
Energy metabolism
Nerve function
Blood formation
Vision
Bone health
Healing
They do not directly give calories like carbs, protein, fat.
